About Me

I bring a unique perspective as a designer, blending my software engineering background and nearly 7 years of experience in the field. My analytical and problem-solving skills, developed through software engineering, help me craft visually appealing and user-friendly design solutions.


I'm also a 3D artist, having collaborated with respected brands like OnePlus and Kitbash3D.


I began designing in 2014-15, and what I created back then is still used by hundreds of thousands of users today. I can't share it here, but feel free to ask, and I'll tell you more.

Now since 2022, I am back to design again.

Let's build something timeless.
